NOTES:

- In early November, a single threat actor registered over 550 domains impersonating booking sites, banks, crypto wallets and restaurants.
- The email POC for all of these registrations is ilotirabec207@gmail[.]com.
- Manual analysis of random domains showed signs of activity before 2024, indicated these are previously-registered domains "dropcaught" by this threat actor.
- Many of these domains are stockpiled and not yet weaponized, while some are currently active in phishing scams.
- 90% of the active domains use Cloudflare for domain fronting, with 10% on shared hosting, possibly revealing the real hosting IP addresses.
- These scams often trick victims into sharing sensitive information like login credentials, financial details and personal identification.
