NOTES:

- We have been monitoring scam campaigns impersonating various cryptocurrency trading platforms.
- These campaigns use domain names that resemble the targeted brand.
- We pivot on the domains we discover and hunt for similar malicious domains using Graph AI.
  -- More info on the Graph AI at https://distill.pub/2021/gnn-intro/
- The below domains all impersonate deribit[.]com and share similar hosting infrastructure. 
- The malicious domains' hosting infrastructure is distinctly different from the legitimate Deribit infrastructure. 
- The malicious domains use both Cloudflare and non-Cloudflare IP addresses.
- These malicious domains often move from one hosting location to another.
- This resulted in 50+ hosting IP addresses in the last 6 months and more than 9000 pDNS hits. 

- The legitimate deribit[.]com domain was originally registered in 2014.
- The malicious domains we discovered impersonating Deribit were first registered in 2023 and 2024.
